## Identifying sv3a Cards Correctly

One practical issue with sv3a: the set was released only in Japanese, and the collector numbers run beyond the 071-card base set count (the overset numbering for SIRs and SARs starts at 072/071). A lot with a card marked only "Garchomp" tells you nothing about whether it's the base rare, the IR, or the SIR. You need all three fields: **name + SV3a + collector number**. Card 085/071 is the SIR worth $80-$120. Card 078/071 is the IR worth $12-$18. Card 005/071 is the base rare worth under $2.
