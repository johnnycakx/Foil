This is the first post on the Foil blog. It also doubles as a smoke test for the MDX pipeline — every custom component the blog supports renders below.

## What this blog is for

The Foil scanner answers one question fast: *what is this Pokémon card actually worth right now?* This blog is for the questions that need more than a single number — how to read a [Japanese set code](/japanese-pokemon-cards-value), why a PSA 9 sometimes outsells a PSA 10, and how to spot the cards that are quietly worth more than the listing.

<Callout variant="info" title="What you'll see here">
Posts skew toward sourcing, condition grading, and Japanese-set deep dives — the corners of the hobby where the data Foil pulls actually changes a buying decision.
</Callout>
