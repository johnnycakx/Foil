Self-hosted here for the Satori/ImageResponse OG runtime as the BODY font of
the share cards (brand-og-unification, 2026-07-02). This is byte-identical to
the fallback TTF next/og itself bundles (node_modules/next/dist/compiled/
@vercel/og/Geist-Regular.ttf) — self-hosting it lets the OG surfaces pass it
EXPLICITLY alongside Shrikhand, because supplying any `fonts` array to
ImageResponse replaces the bundled fallback entirely. The site's page-visible
body text loads the same family via next/font in app/layout.tsx.
