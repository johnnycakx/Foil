**The Big Move — Ethan's Ho-Oh ex (Destined Rivals)**

Ethan's Ho-Oh ex is up 15.6% to $216.33 this week against a $187.11 month-average, and 249 sales means this is not a thin-volume trick. That's real money moving through real transactions. My read is that Destined Rivals is a nostalgia-forward set and the Ethan tie-in likely hits a specific collector nerve that the straight ex cards don't. I'd also bet there's some chase-pull energy still circulating from pack openings, though I can't confirm either cause. When a card clears $216 and posts this kind of move on this kind of volume, the instinct is to chase it. That instinct is usually wrong. You are not buying the opportunity here, you are buying the top of the current run. The floor on this card is probably somewhere well below where it's trading right now, and a week or two of settling will show you where. I'd wait. Let the opening hype exhale, watch whether 249-sale weeks continue or dry up, and look for an entry closer to the $187.11 month-average. The card isn't going away.
