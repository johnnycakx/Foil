## The outreach stack (surround the decision-maker, in order)

1. **Cold DMs** (IG/FB/LinkedIn) — easiest volume lift. Personalize (30s research), attach machine photos,
   never close in DMs; goal = get to a call or stop-in.
2. **Cold calls** — call personal cells first (decision-makers), business line last. **Double-dial** to bypass
   DND; no voicemail until 5–7 days of attempts. For iOS call-screening, say "Hey [Name], it's…" and
   trail off (curiosity). Save the number so callbacks show their name. Log every call in the CRM.
3. **Cold emails** — cheeky/curious subject lines ("I found money on your wall"); say "we" not "I";
   personalize; stress turnkey/zero-cost/compact/wrappable/monthly profit share.
4. **Warm emails** — sent AFTER they ask for info. Ask what they want included, send same-day, pull only
   relevant sections, keep tone warm.
5. **Walk-ins** — where deals close. Calculated (timed via CRM intel), buy something first, bring a **gift
   basket** (packs + drinks + flyer + card), pitch briefly, run AR, get a soft commit. "A 90% close."

**Gatekeeper namedrop:** "Hey, is [Owner] around?" → if not, "When does [Owner] come around nowadays?"
("nowadays" implies you know them) → note the time → calculated walk-in.

## The pitch points (every channel)

Turnkey (we install/stock/service/insure), zero cost + zero risk, compact (jukebox-sized, ~3–4 sq ft),
high-tech touchscreen that educates buyers, Pokémon demand at all-time high, **wrappable to match their
aesthetic**, passive monthly income. Profit split line: "We typically do a 10–15% net profit split" then stay
silent; rebuttal: "it's a value-add, not rent money." Hold ≤30% unless A+ location.

## Closing toolkit (stack these)

1. **Trial period (#1 closer)** — "place it, meet in the middle at ~25% for a 1–2 month trial, review the data
   together, I'll pull it if it's not working." Removes risk; once it's up, they almost never renegotiate.
2. **AR close** — place the machine virtually in their space → shifts them from "do I want this?" to "where
   does it go?" (AR tool: vtmvending.com → pick a machine → View in 3D → View Object in Place.)
3. **Assumptive close** — skip the yes: "How do you prefer to be paid, check or direct deposit?"
4. **Advance commission (power move, sparingly)** — small upfront cash credited against their split, for
   A+ spots.

## CRM discipline (non-negotiable)

Every contact logged: time, who, what was said both ways, objections, personal/biz details, next-contact
date. Notes are how rapport + follow-through happen. Say "reconnect/regroup/check in," never "follow up."
