## Account management (keeps locations for years — the real moat)

- **Never let a machine go out of stock.** Set VTM alerts at ~10% capacity; restock when 3–6 aisles hit it
  (one trip vs three). Keep backstock at home (product takes 3–5 days to arrive).
- **Optimize product mix** so all aisles deplete together; hot pack → bump price or add a slot; dead pack →
  swap, move to mystery aisle.
- **You're a guest.** Clean up every install (no cardboard/screws/drywall dust), restock during dead hours /
  vendor hours, never during rushes. Handle any owner issue same-day ("speed = respect").
- **Read the owner:** chatty → build rapport, gift baskets, check-ins; silent → "out of sight, out of mind,
  paid on time," no unsolicited reports.
- **Free packs to employees** = unofficial sales reps inside each location. Highest-ROI small spend.
- **Holiday gift baskets** (Visa card + packs + note) 1–2×/yr to good locations.
- **Referrals** after 1–6 months of good service = best new-location source (warm, high close rate). Don't
  pay for referrals (cheapens it); just ask.
- Pay **on time, every time** (the single most important retention lever).

## Sourcing product (COGS — drives the margin)

- **Target ≥ $10 profit per pack.** Stock two tiers: premium (~$25 sale / $12+ wholesale) + mid/filler
  (~$15–19 sale / $5–9 wholesale). Indecisive buyers grab the $15; collectors grab the $25; both net $10+.
- **Sources:** (1) blueprint supplier (easiest), (2) **eBay bulk lots** (best value — loose packs, booster
  boxes; avoid ETBs, the per-pack math fails), (3) TCGplayer (price-check + emergency buys), (4) Facebook
  Marketplace (scalper bulk), (5) local card shops (relationship pipeline), (6) retail scalping at drops
  (highest margin, most legwork).
- **Evaluate any lot:** total cost (after tax+ship) ÷ pack count = cost/pack; compare to machine sale price.
- **Hot sets shift constantly** — recent examples: Ascended Heroes, Prismatic Evolutions, Phantasmal
  Flames, White Flare, Black Bolt, Mega Evolutions, Surging Sparks, Destined Rivals. Check trends before
  buying.
- Pricing references: **TCGplayer** (market + history) and **eBay Sold Listings** (true real-world value).
  (Note: John's deal-finder code already wraps both — reusable for an internal pricing tool later.)

## Machine ordering + sizing (recap; full detail in 00-BUSINESS-OVERVIEW)

Mini Wall (8 SKU) / Slim Wall (10) / Mega Wall (15) / Slim Tower 2.0 (24, freestanding). All ship 22mm
coils. High-traffic or far-from-base → go bigger (Slim Tower restocks every ~6 weeks). Wrong machine →
relocate. ~1 in 5 sites need a $20/mo cellular kit for wifi. Machines ship from Cleveland, OH (3–5 wk buffer).

## Ad revenue (second income stream per machine — BUILD-RELEVANT later)

Every machine's touchscreen is a **digital billboard**. VTM sources advertisers (enter each machine's
address in the software); going rate $35–100 **per slot**, 4–6 slots/machine → ~$140–600/mo/machine
potential. Can also sell slots directly to local businesses (anyone who'd buy a billboard) at $50–150/slot,
3–6 month minimums. This is a real future revenue line and a possible **secondary website CTA**
("Advertise on our machines") down the road — not in the first build.
