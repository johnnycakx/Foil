## ⚠️ Two rules that get profiles suspended — don't break them

1. **Business name = the real name only. No keywords.** Use "Foil TCG" — do NOT write "Foil TCG Pokémon
   Card Vending Machines." Keyword-stuffing the name is the #1 suspension cause. Keywords go in the
   *category* and *description*, never the name.
2. **One profile, honest info.** Real phone, real website, real service area. Don't invent a storefront address.

## Verification — video (most common for SABs in 2026)

You'll record **one continuous, unedited video (30+ sec) from the Business Profile app on your phone.**
Since you have no storefront, show proof you operate the business:
- Your branded materials (flyers/business cards), sealed Pokémon pack inventory, your workspace.
- Your LLC / business documents (Foil TCG, LLC) if asked.
- Move slowly, pause 2–3 seconds on each item. Don't stop the recording mid-way.
> Tip: if Google offers video verification, take it — it's faster than waiting on a postcard.

1. **Self-populate the Q&A:** post the host FAQ questions + answers (from `01-HOST-LOCATION-OFFER.md` /
   the live /faq) on the profile so owners see them answered. (Insurance/liability stays off — call topic.)

3. **Reviews are the biggest ranking + credibility lever** — ask every happy host for a Google review as
   you place machines. This is the legitimate way to build the "established" proof.
4. **Keep NAP identical** everywhere (name "Foil TCG", the phone, foiltcg.com) — on the site, GBP, and any
   directory listing. Inconsistency hurts ranking.
